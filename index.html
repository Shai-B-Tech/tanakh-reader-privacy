Privacy Policy — Tanakh Reader

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Information About the Developer

Tanakh Reader is an Android application for reading the Hebrew Bible (Tanakh), developed and operated by Shai Baruch.

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, contact: shayb93g@gmail.com

2. What Information Does the App Collect?

The app does not collect any personal information whatsoever.

The application operates entirely locally on your device and does not share any data with external servers or third parties.

3. What Does the App Store Locally?

The application may store the following locally on your device:

  • Display settings — such as font size and brightness preferences
  • Reading progress — the last chapter and verse you read

This data is stored solely on your personal device and is not transmitted to any external party.

4. Do We Share Information?

No. The developer does not collect, share, or sell any personal information. We have no advertisements, tracking systems, or third-party data sharing.

5. Do You Collect Data from Children?

The application is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If any changes are made to this privacy policy, the updated version will be published at this URL.

This privacy policy was created exclusively for the "Tanakh Reader" Android application.
